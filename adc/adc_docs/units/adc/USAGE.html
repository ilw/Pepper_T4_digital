Application Notes#

System Integration#

Because of the Asynchronous SAR operation there are fast current transients on the digital and analogue supplies. This operation runs internally at around 100 MHz depending on settling speed of the CDAC over PVT. Because of this reason we require a 10 nF decoupling capacitance on the analog supplies AVDD/AGND. This will minimize any conversion errors without having a 100 MHz bandwidth requirement on the LDO.
